About Helixmith

Our Company

On the edge of a major breakthrough
in plasmid DNA-based gene therapy

Being a source of biopharmaceutical innovation, our mission is to tackle the unmet medical needs of patients.

Our flagship product Engensis (VM202) is one of our first success cases with multiple phase 3 studies underway in the US. Engensis is a plasmid DNA encoding a gene capable of regenerating microvasculature and nerve cells. With Engensis, we aim to fundamentally treat neurological, muscular and ischemic diseases in areas previously not addressable with conventional medicine. So far, more than 700 patients have gone through clinical studies using the product with excellent safety profile and promising therapeutic outcomes.

Based on our proprietary technology and know-how from 20 years of gene therapy research, we are researching new approaches to tackle previously untreated diseases. Cell-mediated gene therapy is one of the areas where our effort are put into in ways that could expand CAR-T program to treat solid tumors. We also drive innovation through early stage pipeline products targeting various neuromuscular diseases.

The company is currently headquartered in Seoul, Korea, while our clinical development and manufacturing activities are based in San Diego, US. In particular, we have a DNA production facility through Genopis Inc, an affiliated CMO in San Diego. Genopis is a cGMP ready facility for DNA production that serves both Helixmith and external customers in need of DNA.
