ABOUT HELIXMITH

Our Company

Our mission is to tackle the unmet
medical needs of patients.

Being a source of biopharmaceutical innovation, our mission is to tackle the unmet medical needs of patients.

Sunyoung Kim, DPhil, then professor, and his team of scientists at Seoul National University theorized that certain endogenous genes, if replicated, would restore the function of the human body in ways never seen before. To transform this knowledge into a platform for groundbreaking medical treatments, ViroMedica Pacific, now Helixmith Co., Ltd., was established on the university campus in 1996.

Over the next two decades, the company grew from a university lab start-up

to a pioneer in the area of plasmid DNA gene therapy, with a particular emphasis on diseases associated with neurological, muscular or ischemic problems. Our proprietary and patented technologies have uniquely positioned us to develop a pipeline of new, first-in-class, and breakthrough treatments.

Headquartered in Seoul and San Diego

Headquartered in Seoul and San Diego, Helixmith today has established Genopis Inc. in San Diego that will serve as the site for cGMP manufacturing operation.
